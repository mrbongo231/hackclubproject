How to get the Trident Rod in Roblox Fisch

Why should I get trident rod?

One of the best middlegame rods

The Trident Rod is considered a great mid-game fishing rod because it offers a high luck stat, unique passive abilities that increase catch chances, and can be obtained at a relatively accessible point in the game, making it particularly valuable for players progressing through the middle stages of gameplay.

Stab ability

The Trident Rod is notable for its ability to randomly 'stab' a fish when reeling it, briefly stunning it and advancing minigame progress. This also ignores any debuffs in Progress Speed.

Fischipedia

Atlantean mutation

Atlantean is a Mutation which multiplies a Fish's C$ value by 3x. Visually, it causes the fish to turn a dark cyan color, with sparkles resembling that of the Sparkling attribute. Atlantean fish can only be obtained via the Trident Rod.

How to get the trident rod


To get the trident rod you need to

  1. Complete the Desolate Deep Bestiary Desolate Deep
  2. Collect 5 Enchant Relics Enchant relics
  3. Earn at least 150,000 Tokens, and then access the underwater shrine in Desolate Deep to purchase the rod using the required relics
